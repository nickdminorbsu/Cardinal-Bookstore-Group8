Welcome to Our Website!

Welcome to Cardinal Bookstore, your cozy corner for new and used books. We are a proud, college bookstore dedicated to the love of reading and learning. Our digital "home" here is a simple place to learn more about us and see what we have to offer.

Please use the links above to navigate our site. You can learn our story, browse a *selection* of our in-store catalog, and find out how to get in touch with us.

We believe in the simple pleasure of a physical book, so you won't find any "buy now" buttons here. We invite you to visit us in person to find your next great read!

About Cardinal Bookstore

Cardinal Bookstore was established in 1995 by Ball State University. This small store on the college campus has grown into the beloved community hub it is today.

Our mission is simple: to improve business in operations and customer experience. We want to make sure that everything is easier for the customer.

We don't have fancy computers or a big database. We just have a lot of books and a lot of passion. Come in, say hello, and get lost in the stacks.
